Biography

I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Software Engineering. During undergraduate years, I received multiple awards such as Zuguang Ma Scholarship (<0.1%) and National Scholarship. For more details you can refer to my cv.

Currently, I am a research intern at Knowledge Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia, advised by Börje Karlsson. I also enjoyed learning from other awesome guys, such as Jin-Ge Yao.

I am always open for research collboration opportunities. (e.g., Internship, Research Assistant) Don't hesitate to contact with me if you are interested.

My research interests lie in, but not limited to Graph Neural Networks, Robust Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning. I aim to bridge theory and practice by conducting impactful interdisciplinary research, i.e., developing new theories and understandings of Machine Learning, and applying them to solving real problems in graph-like systems, e.g. Computer Network, Knowledge Graph, Social Network and etc, empowering AI to improve the quality of people’s lives.
